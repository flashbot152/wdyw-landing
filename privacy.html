Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2025

wdyw? ("we", "us", or "our") operates the What Do You Want? mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Email address when you create an account.
  • Profile information: Nickname and profile photo you choose to upload.
  • Location data: Your approximate location (with your permission) to find nearby restaurants. We do not store your location history.
  • Usage data: Restaurants you swipe on, your preferences (cuisine type, price range, distance), and session activity when swiping with friends.
  • Device information: Push notification token for sending match alerts.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the App experience
  • To find nearby restaurants based on your location
  • To enable friend swiping sessions and mutual match notifications
  • To sync your preferences and liked restaurants across sessions
  • To send push notifications about mutual matches (only with your permission)

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only as follows:

  • Google Places API: Your location is sent to Google to fetch nearby restaurant results. Google's Privacy Policy applies.
  • Firebase (Google): We use Firebase for authentication, database, and push notifications. Google's Privacy Policy applies.
  • Friend sessions: When you start a joint swiping session, your swipe results for that session are shared with your invited friend only.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time from the Profile screen in the App, which permanently removes all your data from our systems.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can delete it.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Delete your account and all associated data (via the Profile screen)
  • Withdraw location permissions at any time via your device settings
  • Opt out of push notifications via your device settings

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including Firebase Authentication and encrypted data transmission. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the App or by email. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
